How this works

When you ask a question, REM searches everything you've saved, finds the most relevant pieces, and connects them into a clear answer. The more depth you choose, the more of your data it reads through. Leave the question blank to get a full pattern scan across everything.

🎯 How goal-filtering works

When you ask a question, REM searches your notes, emails, and saved items — and ranks what's most relevant to your goals. It then thinks through the top results and gives you an answer built from your own data, not made up.

1 · RetrieveSearches everything you've saved, scored by recency + your goals
2 · FilterLow-relevance items dropped, not guessed from
3 · GroundClaude given relevant pieces as context — answers cite real data
4 · SurfaceInsights tagged by type (pattern, connection, synthesis) + confidence score
Results are ranked by relevance to your goals — the more goals you've set, the sharper the output.
