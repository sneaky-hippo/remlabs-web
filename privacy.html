Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

REM Labs ("we", "us", "our") operates remlabs.ai and the REM Labs platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information about you.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Account information: Name and email address when you sign up with Google OAuth.
  • Memory content: The memories, notes, and data you store on the platform.
  • Usage data: How you use the platform, which features you use, and when.
  • API keys: Keys generated for your account to authenticate API requests.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the REM Labs platform
  • To run Dream Engine synthesis cycles on your memory data
  • To generate your Morning Brief and Intelligence Score
  • To authenticate you and secure your account
  • To respond to your requests and provide support

Memory Data

Your memory content is processed by our Dream Engine to generate insights, patterns, and your Morning Brief. This processing happens on our infrastructure. We do not sell your memory data to third parties or use it to train AI models without your explicit consent.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. We implement security best practices including rate limiting, access controls, and regular security reviews.

Cookies and Sessions

We use HTTP-only session cookies to keep you logged in. These cookies are not accessible to JavaScript and are cleared when you log out. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth for authentication. When you sign in with Google, Google may collect information as described in their privacy policy. Our backend infrastructure runs on Railway. Your data is subject to their infrastructure security policies.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your memories at any time through the Memory Hub. To delete your account, contact us at privacy@remlabs.ai.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@remlabs.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

Children

REM Labs is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our platform or emailing you.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at privacy@remlabs.ai.
